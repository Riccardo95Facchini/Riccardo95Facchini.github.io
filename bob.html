Bob the Chameleon

Unity 2D platform adventure game.

Story

Bob always dreamed of walking around Madagascar, but never got the courage, until today! He decides he will embark on a journey alone, exploring all that his home country has to offer and eating all the bugs he can on the way to his destination.
He must be careful though, because snakes, wasps, eagles, spikes and pitfalls are very common around here and he would like to keep all of his limbs.
Join Bob in his adventure, in this journey throughout Madagascar, and donâ€™t forget to eat while on the road!

Swing Bob, Swing!

Use Bob's tongue to swing over pits and cover long distances by reeling and using momentum to swing to the other side.

Hide from predators

Bob is a Chameleon, and as such he can hide from predators by changing his color to match the background of bushes and trees.

Become the predator!

Eat harmless insects to get a higher score.

Summary

Bob the Chameleon was created for the Videogame Design course in Politecnico di Milano during the academic year 2018-2019.
It was developed with the help of a level designer, a sound designer and a graphic designer that designed Bob itself.
All other assets used were free assets on the Unity Asset Store.
